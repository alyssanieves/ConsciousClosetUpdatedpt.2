Welcome to ConsciousCloset

Your go-to platform for sustainable fashion and thrift store discovery.

Our Mission

At ConsciousCloset, our mission is to empower consumers to make more sustainable fashion choices. By offering an easy-to-use platform that evaluates the sustainability of clothing brands and connects users with nearby thrift stores, we aim to promote mindful shopping and a greener future for fashion.

Rating Scale

  • 1: Not Good – The brand lacks transparency and fails to demonstrate any significant efforts toward ethical practices or sustainability.
  • 2: Somewhat Ethical – The brand shows minimal commitment to sustainability, using limited sustainable materials but lacking a comprehensive approach.
  • 3: Moderately Ethical – The brand is somewhat transparent about its practices and makes efforts to reduce its environmental impact.
  • 4: Ethical – The brand follows responsible practices, ensuring fair treatment of workers and a focus on sustainable sourcing.
  • 5: Exceptional – The brand is a leader in sustainability, actively working to minimize its environmental footprint and promote ethical practices throughout its supply chain.

About Us & Our Technology

ConsciousCloset is dedicated to simplifying the journey toward sustainable fashion. We believe that transparency is key to making informed choices. Our platform uses advanced technology to evaluate the sustainability of brands based on their online presence and practices.

Simply input the URL of a clothing brand, and our system analyzes available data to provide you with a sustainability rating. We look at factors like material sourcing, manufacturing processes, and transparency to ensure you get an accurate picture of each brand’s commitment to sustainability.
